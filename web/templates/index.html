DogCare AI — Smart Dog Health & Activity Suite

Predict your dog's weight, estimate daily calorie needs, and classify activity level (Hyperactive · Normal · Low activity). Lightweight models — suitable for local demos and prototypes.

How to use

  1. Go to the Weight page to predict weight from breed, age, and measurements.
  2. Go to the Calorie page to estimate daily calories (use predicted or provided weight).
  3. Go to the Activity page to classify activity level from play / walk / sleep inputs.
  4. Use the BMI page for a BMI-like score and health risk probabilities (obesity, joint, heart).

Notes

  • All user inputs and predictions can be saved to MongoDB for analytics and history.
  • This is a demo — results are educational and not a substitute for veterinary advice.
